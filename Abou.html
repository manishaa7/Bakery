About Us

Our Story

I am Aroha, learnt the art of making pastries from my mother, she had a small bakery shop in Dunedin. I later moved to Auckland and met my husband Taiko, who also loved baking. Together we decided to establish a business inspired by our passion for baking in 2019. We set up Aroha Bakery in Henderson, Auckland.


Our Bread

Aroha is dedicated to producing breads using traditionally baking techniques, handmade bread, pastries and more with simple, high quality ingredients. All baked locally in our wood fired brick oven.
